What We Do

We bridge the digital divide by transforming used computers into powerful tools for education and opportunity. Our mission is to make technology accessible to everyone while promoting environmental sustainability through computer recycling.

Donate

Individuals and businesses give us their used computers, helping to reduce e-waste while supporting our mission to bridge the digital divide.

Refurbish

Devices are securely wiped, repaired, and upgraded to ensure they meet modern performance standards while maintaining data security.

Distribute

Restored computers are donated to students, nonprofits, and families in need, providing them with the tools they need to succeed in today's digital world.

How to Donate

We accept laptops and desktop computers less than 10 years old. Your donation can make a real difference in someone's life.

Accepted Devices

  • Laptops and desktop computers
  • Less than 10 years old
  • With power cord, if available

Security & Data Protection

We use industry-standard data wiping software to securely erase all data from donated computers. We can provide certificate of data destruction for your records if needed.
